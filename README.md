installation instructions:
1: download kopernicus planetary system modifier
2: download the mod
3: drag mod into gamedata
4: enjoy

what does the mod do?
eve: has dres as a moon
kerbin: has ike as a moon
laythe: now a planet, orbits slightly beyond kerbin with 1 moon, moho
duna: lost ike for gilly and bop
pol: lonely between jool and duna
jool: traded laythe, bop, and gilly for minmus and eeloo
